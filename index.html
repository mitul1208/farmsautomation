We at Farm Automation

Automating Your Greenhouse to provide local and remote control

Our team is experienced in installing open source and secure IoT devices that are cost effictive, relaiable and easy to maintain.

Today automation is crucial for growers looking to modernize and optimize for sustainable crops. Automating your daily grow operation gives you more efficient way of managing the ideal climate, necessary nutritions to your crops and piece of mind to you through effective notificatons.

Our Services Includes

  • Assessment of your current grow facility
  • Controller setup and installation
  • Integrate your existing device to the platform
  • Training growers on Automation Interface
Learn More

Decrease your Labor Cost

By reducing the amount of work that needs to be done manually,you can decrease your labor costs or allow you to free up workers to focus on other areas of importance. Perfect if you are looking to expand your business or introduce new skills to your workers.

Increase quality and yield

If you don’t measure you can’t improve. Automation will give you the knowledge to increase quality and yield, learn from previous crop cycles, and give you better return on investment (ROI).

Be Accurate

Know exactly what’s happening in your greenhouse and make data driven decisions based on facts and not assumptions. The technology and equipment exist to benefit you as the grower!

Monitoring and Security

Our custom solution gives actionable notifications, access control to high security doors and CCTV cameras.
