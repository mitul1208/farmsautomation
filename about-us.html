Our team is full of technology professionals and futurist nerds. We enjoy expanding technology in useful and meaningful ways. We could think of no better way to apply our collective skillsets to benefit society than to give power to the various farming communities.

We leverage the internet of things to help farmers maximize the potential of their operation. We use fully integrated devices to provide automated solutions to your grow facility with cost effcient and advanced hardware and software technologies.

With Farm Assistant, our farmers are empowered to leave the farm with peace of mind and monitor all aspects of the facility from remote locations.

We aim to integrate any opportunity for automation into our platform. It's common that our clients collaborate with our automation experts to bring visions for improvement to reality. If our customers have ideas or requests for new integrations we are excited to help expand our mutual capabilities!

FOCUS ON STAKEHOLDERS

Our philosophy at Farm Automation rejects the common narratives that many businesses have fallen into. We are extremely forward thinking. We serve our customers and their interests. Additionally, our employees are stakeholders and we treat each other as such. We focus our efforts on our core competencies and how they can help farming operations across the board.

VENDOR AGNOSTIC

We provide turnkey solutions to unlock the potential of your operation. We operate on a vendor agnostic approach - this means that we can synchronize your entire operation with devices you've already purchased. We will also work to identify areas of your operation that can be automated or controlled through the Farm Automation platform.

DATA OWNERSHIP

We believe that the businesses and industries that will survive, thrive, and compete are going to leverage features like automation, data analytics, and system level robustness. At Farm Automation, data protection is one of our top priorities. Our platform has end-to-end encryption and your data is stored on your servers at YOUR facility. Unlike many tech companies out there, we believe that your data exists to serve YOU. Farmers making data driven decisions should have the ability to monetize that data in tangible ways to improve the outcome their operation. We can provide insights through data science to improve throughput and lower overhead cost.!

PRODUCT FOCUSED

Our devotion to quality and our system's focus on autonomous farming is one of our core values. We take seriously the robustness of our Farmers' operation. Our goal is to provide revolutionary IoT services so that cultivators can focus on their crops, rather than the facility itself.
